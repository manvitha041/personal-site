Geofree coming soon

Mobile App • Branding • Personas • Wireframes • Flowcharts • User Interface • Prototyping
Project Overview
Geofree is a mobile that allows users to post objects that they no longer need or want to giveaway for whatever the reason. Those in need of free items can search through the mobile for desired objects or come across with new free items near their location.
My Contributions
  • Collaborated with a team of designers, developers, backend and data science members to create a web app for users to post and search for free items.
  • Played an active role in all stages of the design process, taking the Initiation & led the development of the app’s branding and messaging.
  • Utilized best practices in UX design to ensure a seamless user experience. Conducted user research and testing to gather feedback and iterate on design concepts.
