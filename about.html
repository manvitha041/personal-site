I enjoy figuring out how to make things work better.


from Fashion Designer to Web Developer to UI/UX Designer


As a designer, I care about the details and consider that how things look and work on the screen is very important for creating effective, intuitive interfaces. That’s why I use prototyping as my weapon of choice in thinking through interaction models and new possibilities.

My professional career began with designing and running my mom’s ethnic Indian clothing boutique, which I still enjoy doing. This experience has enhanced my creativity and aesthetic sense, as well as my ability to create visually appealing designer wear.

As a web developer, I have created several web applications that sparked my interest in interface design and problem-solving. I enjoy applying my understanding and learnings to create user-friendly and effective solutions. My passion and goal is to become a UI/UX designer.

I thrive on learning new things — I like discovering new ways to reach familiar destinations. I really love cooking and experimenting with different cuisines. Once, I made a fusion dish of Indian Curry on a Pizza that turned out surprisingly delicious.I also really enjoy travelling around and enjoy seeing things from a fresh perspective :)
